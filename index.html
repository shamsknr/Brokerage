💱 Currency Derivatives (NSE)
Currency F&O trades on NSE-CDS segment. No STT or CTT. Exchange charges: Futures ₹0.35/lakh turnover | Options ₹2/lakh premium. No DP charges. SEBI ₹10/crore.
Select Futures or Options; enter lot size (e.g. USD/INR = 1000 units per lot).
🥇 Commodity Derivatives (MCX)
MCX Commodity — Non-agricultural futures attract CTT 0.01% on sell side (gold, silver, crude oil, metals). Agri commodities are CTT-exempt. Exchange charge: ~₹2.10/lakh turnover. No STT, no DP charges. SEBI ₹10/crore.
Select contract type and enter values based on MCX lot sizes (e.g. Gold Mini = 100g, Crude Oil = 100 bbl).
Margin Trading Facility (MTF)
MTF lets you buy stocks by paying only a margin, with the broker funding the rest. You pay interest on the funded amount daily until you square off.
Total Cost = Delivery brokerage + STT + Exchange charges + MTF Interest (annual rate ÷ 365 × days × funded amount) + GST on interest.
Securities Lending & Borrowing Mechanism (SLBM)
SLBM lets shareholders lend their securities to earn a lending fee, or borrow securities for short selling.
Lender: Earns gross income minus platform charges.  Borrower: Pays lending fee plus platform charges.
Charges: SLBM Txn Fee (0.10% of transaction value) + GST (18%) + SEBI charges (₹0.10/lakh).
🔒 Pledge / Unpledge
Pledge lets you use your existing stock holdings as collateral to get extra margin for trading — without selling your shares.
Charges apply per ISIN per request (regardless of quantity). GST (18%) is added on top of each broker's fee.
Collateral Margin = Stock Value × (1 − Haircut%). SEBI mandates 50% cash + 50% collateral for F&O margins.
🏦 Demat Account AMC Comparison
Annual Maintenance Charge (AMC) is a yearly fee to keep your Demat account active. Account Opening Charge is a one-time fee to open trading + demat accounts.
AMC values shown are standard plan (excl. GST). GST 18% extra on AMC. BSDA accounts (holdings ≤₹4L) get ₹0 AMC per SEBI mandate.
Note: Zerodha accounts opened on/after 1 Jun 2026 get free 1st year AMC. Most brokers currently offer free account opening.
